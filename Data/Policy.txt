Privacy Policy
At Cashmir Biotech Pvt Ltd, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and other applicable laws in India. This policy applies to all customers, partners, and visitors engaging with our services, website, or products.

Information We Collect
Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details provided during purchases, inquiries, or account creation.
Professional Information: Business details, such as company name, designation, or industry, for B2B interactions or partnerships.
Transaction Data: Details of purchases, orders, payments, and returns.
Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data when you visit our website (www.cashmirbiotech.com).
Biotech-Specific Data: Information related to product preferences, research interests, or specific requirements for biotech products, where applicable.
Sensitive Personal Data: In certain cases, we may collect sensitive data (e.g., health-related data for specific biotech products), only with your explicit consent and as permitted by law.
How We Collect Information
Direct Interactions: When you place orders, contact us via email (support@cashmirbiotech.com), phone (+91-9103 524624), or forms on our website.
Automated Technologies: Cookies, web beacons, and analytics tools to track website usage and improve user experience.
Third Parties: Business partners, distributors, or service providers, with your consent or as per contractual agreements.
How We Use Your Information
To process and fulfill orders, deliver products, and manage returns or refunds.
To communicate with you regarding product updates, promotions, or customer support.
To improve our products, services, and website functionality based on user feedback and analytics.
To comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including tax, safety, and biotech industry standards.
To conduct research and development, ensuring our biotech products meet your needs.
To protect against fraud, unauthorized transactions, or security breaches.
Legal Basis for Processing
Consent: Where you have explicitly agreed to provide data for specific purposes (e.g., marketing or sensitive data).
Contractual Necessity: To fulfill orders or provide services as per agreements.
Legal Obligation: To comply with Indian laws, such as the DPDP Act or tax regulations.
Legitimate Interests: For business purposes like improving services, provided it does not override your rights.
Sharing and Disclosure
We may share your data with:

Service Providers: Trusted third parties for payment processing, shipping, or IT services, bound by confidentiality agreements.
Regulatory Authorities: As required by law or to comply with regulatory standards in the biotech industry.
Business Partners: For collaborative research or product development, with your consent.
We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security
Storage: Your data is stored securely on servers located in India or with compliant cloud providers adhering to DPDP Act standards.
Security Measures: We use encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
Retention: We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined or as required by law. After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.
Your Rights
Under the DPDP Act, you have the following rights:

Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
Restriction: Request restriction of data processing in certain cases.
Objection: Object to processing for marketing or other purposes based on legitimate interests.
Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another entity, where applicable.
Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time for data processed based on consent.
To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@cashmirbiotech.com.

Cookies and Tracking
Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For details, refer to our Cookie Policy on our website.

International Data Transfers
If data is transferred outside India (e.g., for cloud storage), we ensure compliance with DPDP Act requirements and use safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses to protect your data.

Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing personal information.

Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on our website with the revision date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
